Sentimentu Feelings

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter

Vitalina dos Santos

Aurelio da Costa, Vitalina dos Santos, Criscencia R. Da Costa Viana

Tetum

Level 1

Ha’u nia fuan sente buat barak.

My heart feels a lot of things.

Ha’u sente kontente bainhira ha’u nia avo-mae konta istória mai ami iha kalan.

I feel happy when my granny tells us stories in the evening.

Ha’u sente beik bainhira ha’u halimar ho ha’u nia kolega.

I feel silly when I play with my friend.

Ha’u sente la di’ak bainhira ha’u nia papa dehan nia la iha osan.

I feel bad when my dad says he does not have money.

Ha’u sente domin bainhira ha’u nia mama hakohak ha’u.

I feel loved when my mom gives me a hug.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Vitalina dos Santos
Read by: Aurelio da Costa, Vitalina dos Santos, Criscencia R. Da Costa Viana
Language: Tetum
Level: Level 1
