Tsitsi Hair

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter

David Sani Mwanza

Christine Mwanza

Nyanja

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Thuli alo ndi tsitsi yaifupi.

Thuli has short hair.

Ana ali ndi tsitsi yaitali.

Anna has long hair.

Keti ali ndi tsitsi yaitali koposa.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Zama wamanga tsitsi yake.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Baba ali ndi ndevu.

Baba has a beard.

Zanele wasakula tsitsi yake.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Thabo wajuwa tsitsi yake.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Themba wagela tsitsi yake.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: David Sani Mwanza
Read by: Christine Mwanza
Language: Nyanja
Level: Level 1
Source: Hair from African Storybook
