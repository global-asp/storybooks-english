Vejrbogen Weather book

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter

Kim Sandvad West

Danish

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Det er tørt.

It is dry.

Solen er varm.

The sun is hot.

Det blæser.

It is windy.

Det er overskyet.

It is cloudy.

Det er koldt.

It is cold.

Det regner.

It is raining.

Det tordner.

There is thunder.

Jeg ser en regnbue.

I see a rainbow.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Kim Sandvad West
Language: Danish
Level: Level 1
Source: Weather book from African Storybook
