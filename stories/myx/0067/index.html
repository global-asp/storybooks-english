Khuuteekha Cooking

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter

Milton Eridad Shissa

Lumasaaba

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Indi khuhaasa kapwondi.

I peel the potatoes.

Nakhalaka kapiki.

I chop a cabbage.

Naasyaka tsi karoti.

I grate the carrots.

Nasiinga kamakaanda nabutaama.

I wash the beans.

Naakhalaka piliganya.

I cut the butternut.

Naakhalaka ni sukuma.

I chop the spinach.

Maayi wase waakhalaka bitungulu.

My mom chops the onions.

Bitungulu bindisa kamasika nga balikho babikhalaka.

Onions make me cry when they are chopped.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Milton Eridad Shissa
Language: Lumasaaba
Level: Level 1
Source: Cooking from African Storybook
