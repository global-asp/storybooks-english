Omuliro Fire

Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Amos Mubunga Kambere

Lukhonzo

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Samalira, omuliro!

Look, fire!

Omuliro akahisaya.

Fire burns.

Omuliro akatsumba.

Fire cooks.

Omuliro akahuhaya.

Fire gives warmth.

Omuliro akaletha ekyakakalha.

Fire gives light.

Samalira, omuliro!

Look, fire!

Omuliro uwene.

Fire is wonderful.

Kandi awithe obuthoki bunene.

And it is powerful.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Amos Mubunga Kambere
Language: Lukhonzo
Level: Level 1
Source: Fire from African Storybook
